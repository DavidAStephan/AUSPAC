## Resolved divergences

**eps_pQ structural channel (Phase S FR-BDF replication, 2026-05-16).** Pre-Phase-S
AU showed +0.15% ln_Q at Q5 vs FR-BDF −0.45% at Q8 — sign-level divergence.
Root cause: the E-SAT reduced-form Phillips equation `eq_au_phillips`
lacked the `piQ → pi_au` passthrough channel that wp736 §3.1.1 has (FR-BDF
puts π_Q directly on the LHS of the E-SAT Phillips). **Phase S resolves
this structurally**: added the FR-BDF deflator channels `α_pc·(piQ−pibar_au)
+ β_pc_m·(pi_m−pibar_au) + γ_oil·dln_pcom` to `eq_au_phillips` across all
8 .mod files. MCMC re-run improved MHM by +1.62 nats (Phase R −790.72 →
Phase S −789.10). Post-fix: pi_au responds +0.119 qpp on impact, Taylor
rule tightens, output gap turns negative Q8. Real-GDP level ln_Q still
positive throughout — substantive AU finding traced to high AU policy
smoothing (λ_i = 0.96 vs FR-BDF 0.89) plus weak AU piQ→CPI passthrough
(α_pc = 0.17 vs FR-BDF 0.71). See [WP §4.4.0 / §6.3.5](AUSPAC_WORKING_PAPER.md)
for full diagnostic.
